About us

Hello everyone,

We are the Robert Gordons University Craft Beer Society.

Here at the Craft Beer Society we love to enjoy great beer with equally great company.

Everyone is welcome, whether you are an avid beer lover, are just getting started in the world of craft beer of just want to have a few drinks with some friends.

We hold weekly meeting at brewdog Aberdeen, where we try their newly brewed craft beers along with any other beers you enjoy

If you are interested in joining please make your way to the sign up page and fill out the form.
